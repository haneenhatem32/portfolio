UI Design / Mobile App

Booky App Case Study

Redesigning the digital reading experience for the modern user.

Role

Lead UI/UX Designer

Timeline

3 Months

Tools

Figma, FigJam, Notion

Overview

Booky is a conceptual reading app designed to make discovering, tracking, and reading books a seamless and highly social experience. The primary challenge was to design an interface that felt cozy and immersive while providing powerful organizational tools for avid readers.

Through iterative design and user testing, we landed on a vibrant yet clean aesthetic that highlights book covers and user progress.

The Challenge

Users reported feeling overwhelmed by existing reading apps, which often prioritize store features over the reading experience itself. The goal was to create a "digital bookshelf" that felt personal.

  • • Simplify the discovery process for new books.
  • • Create an engaging reading tracking system.
  • • Design a comfortable dark mode for night readers.

Final Design

The final high-fidelity mockups focus on large, beautiful typography and rich imagery. A subtle glassmorphism effect was used for overlays to maintain context without sacrificing legibility.
